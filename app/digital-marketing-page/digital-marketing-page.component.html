Case Study: Search Engine Marketing for a Heritage Pharma Brand

In 2018, we had the opportunity to work with a heritage pharmaceutical brand that had been around for several decades. Not only did we help them to build a web application for their medication ordering and inventory management, but they also engaged us to help them grow their presence online.
